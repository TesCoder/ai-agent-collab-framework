# Naming conventions (template)

Keep naming consistent across:
- analytics events + params
- content slugs
- report headings
- asset filenames

## Analytics

- Events: `snake_case` (example: `guide_download`)
- Params: `snake_case` (example: `cta_location`)

## Files

- Reports: `REPORTS_ROOT/daily/YYYY-MM-DD-update.md`
- Inbox: `REPORTS_ROOT/inbox/Agent<AGENT_INSTANCE_ID>_YYYY-MM-DD.md`
